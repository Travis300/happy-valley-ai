Hi, I'm Luke Surovec.

I'm the founder of Happy Valley AI — a one-person studio building custom AI chatbots for businesses that want to move faster without hiring more headcount.

My background spans cybersecurity, software development, and years of hands-on customer-facing work. That combination matters: I understand how systems are built and I've lived the operational side — the repetitive customer questions, the scheduling back-and-forth, the leads that slip through the cracks. I've seen exactly what wastes time, and I know how to fix it with AI.

I started Happy Valley AI because most businesses don't need an enterprise AI platform — they need one well-built tool that handles the specific thing slowing them down. I build that tool, deploy it fast, and keep improving it over time.

Every chatbot is custom — trained on your business, your tone, your customers. You work directly with me, start to finish. No account managers. No hand-offs. No templates.
