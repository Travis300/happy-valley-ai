// Common Questions

FAQ

Is there a setup fee?
No. Every plan is all-inclusive — you pay one flat monthly rate and we handle everything from build to deployment. No hidden fees, no one-time charges.
How quickly can my chatbot go live?
Most chatbots go live within 48 hours of your discovery call. We'll collect your business info, train the bot, and have it running on your site in under two days.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, anytime. Just reach out and we'll adjust your plan at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no friction.
What happens if I exceed my monthly conversations?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit and discuss upgrading. Your chatbot won't suddenly go offline — we'll always give you a heads up first.
Do I own the chatbot?
The chatbot is built and maintained by us, and your subscription covers ongoing hosting, updates, and support. Think of it like a managed service — we keep it running smoothly so you don't have to.
What platforms does it work on?
We build chatbots for websites (any platform), and can integrate with tools like Slack, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and more depending on your plan. Ask us about your specific setup on the discovery call.
